This mod adds a Godzamok Burst button, configurable building list, and pause/step controls to Cookie Clicker.

How to load

  1. Open Cookie Clicker in your browser.
  2. Go to Options ‚Üí Mods ‚Üí Manage mods ‚Üí Load mod from URL.
  3. Paste this link:
    https://astarogh.github.io/CookieClickerMod/godzi-helper.js
  4. Click OK and the mod will load.

Features

  • Burst button: sell & rebuy selected buildings for Godzamok stacks
  • Configurable: choose which buildings, rebuy delay, etc.
  • Pause & Step controls (P hotkey to pause, O to step)
  • Settings persist between sessions
